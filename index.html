// QUESTIONS

COMMON QUESTIONS

What version of Minecraft do I need?
EVBM PK CIV runs on Minehut and supports Minecraft Java Edition. Most recent versions work — just add evbm.minehut.gg and connect.
Is it free to play?
Yes — 100% free. Every rank, every course, every battle is earned through gameplay. No pay-to-win. The grind is the reward.
What is a parkour battle?
Two players face off. Player 1 attempts a jump. If they land it, Player 2 must match it. Miss and you lose a life. Three lives each — last one standing wins.
Is progress saved when I log off?
Absolutely. Your rank, achievements, and progress are permanently saved. Log off and come back whenever — your civilization waits.
